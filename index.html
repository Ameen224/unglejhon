About

UNCLE JHON

Uncle John has very large range of products and packagings in the ice cream segment of their business. Uncle John was founded in the year 1995 and Kreemfoods (p)Ltd is the present owner of the brand name.

Read more
Serving Kerala State Government as a Senior Bureaucrat for a long period, Mr. M C John took the great step of pursuing his dream to become an entrepreneur after voluntary retirement and transformed into an ‘Icon of Ice Cream Industry’ in due course. After establishing South India's pioneering ice cream brand - Uncle John, new brand additions like Skei were introduced in the coming years by Kreemfoods (P) Ltd founded by himself. Uncle John immediately became a fast-selling ice cream brand with outstanding taste and impeccable quality.
